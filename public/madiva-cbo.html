Who We Are

A Grassroots CBO Built
From the Ground Up

MADIVA CBO is a community-based organization rooted in Bonchari, Kisii County. We believe in the power of local communities to lift themselves — and we provide the skills, knowledge, and platforms to make that happen.

From hairdressing studios to financial boardrooms, our beneficiaries go on to build dignified livelihoods and lead their communities. We work in close collaboration with SHOFCO,Uwezo fund,Youth funds, International Solidarity Foundation, Volunteers Network and NGAAF to expand our reach and deepen our impact.

What We Do

Our Programs

More than Six powerful programs designed to equip community members with real skills and lasting opportunities.

Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy

Professional vocational training in modern hairdressing techniques and beauty therapy, equipping participants with marketable skills for self-employment.

Vocational Ongoing Intake

Financial Literacy

Practical financial education covering savings, budgeting, investments, and how to grow a small business — tailored for grassroots entrepreneurs.

Education Ongoing

Economic Empowerment

Supporting individuals in identifying and developing income-generating activities, with mentorship and community-backed microfinance support.

Empowerment Ongoing

Youth Leadership

Youth Voice & Empowerment Project — building confident, capable young leaders through civic engagement, mentorship, and community action.

Youth Active

Excellence Recognition

Celebrating and rewarding community members who demonstrate outstanding achievement, resilience, and service to their communities.

Partnership and Collaborations

In partnership with Shining Hope for Communities, Uwezo Fund, Youth Fund, NGAAF, Volunteer network and ISF we co-design and co-deliver programs that align with Kenya's national development goals.
